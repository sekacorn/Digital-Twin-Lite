# Disclaimers - Digital Twin Lite

## Primary Disclaimer

**This product is for educational and wellness purposes only and does not provide medical advice.**

## Full Disclaimer Text

Digital Twin Lite is a wellness simulation tool designed for educational purposes only. It is not intended to diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any disease or medical condition.

The predictions generated by this tool are based on simplified rule-based models and are meant to illustrate general directional trends only. They are not clinically validated and should not be relied upon for making health decisions.

Always consult with a qualified healthcare professional before making changes to your diet, exercise routine, or lifestyle based on any information provided by this tool.

## Data Disclaimer

All data entered into this tool is stored locally and is not transmitted to external servers. The tool does not collect personally identifiable health information for any purpose beyond generating simulations within the application.

## Accuracy Disclaimer

The simulation engine uses simplified models that do not account for individual medical conditions, medications, genetic factors, metabolic variations, or other personal health variables. Actual outcomes will vary significantly from predictions.
