# Product Vision - Digital Twin Lite

## Vision Statement

Digital Twin Lite is a wellness and educational prediction tool that helps users understand how their daily habits affect their health outcomes over time.

## What It Is

- A personal wellness simulator
- An educational tool for understanding habit-outcome relationships
- A directional trend predictor based on simple rules

## What It Is NOT

- NOT a medical device or diagnostic tool
- NOT a substitute for professional medical advice
- NOT a clinically validated prediction system

## Target User

Health-curious individuals who want to visualize how changes in sleep, diet, exercise, and hydration might impact their weight and energy levels over time.

## Core Value Proposition

"See your future self" — input your daily habits and get a visual projection of where those habits are likely to take you over 7, 30, or 90 days.
